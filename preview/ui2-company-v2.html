§1Business DNA

What does this company actually do?

It digs limestone, burns it into clinker, grinds the clinker with fly ash, and sells the result in 50 kg bags to builders within roughly 300 km of each plant.

Cement is heavy relative to its price, so freight decides who can compete for a given customer. That one fact explains most of what follows: plant locations are the strategy, and a plant 400 km away is not a competitor.

FY25 annual report, pp. 14–19. Verified 18 Jul 2026.
